Kobe Bryant

Life of Kobe Bryant

  • 1978 - Kobe Bryant was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
    • Bryant spent a part of his childhood in Italy due to his parents moving there
  • 1982 - He was enrolled into Lower Merion High School
    • Colleges began to take notice of Kobe due to his great performances in his junior and senior years
  • 1996 - Kobe was drafted by the Charlotte Hornets, but then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers at the age of 17 years
  • 2000 - He won his first NBA championship and consecutively won his next two championships in the next two years
  • 2006 - The Lakers were playing the Toronto Raptors on the 22nd of January. In that game Kobe scored 81 points, which is the 2nd highest number of points scored by an NBA player in NBA history
  • 2008 - Kobe Bryant was awarded MVP of the 2007-2008 season
  • 2009 - He won his 4th championship and was final MVP for the first time
  • 2010 - Bryant won his 5th championship against the Boston Celtics
  • 2016 - Kobe retired from the NBA at the end of the 2015-2016 season
  • 2018 - Bryant won an Oscar for his animated film Dear Basketball
  • 2020 - On January 26, Kobe Bryant, his daughter and seven others died in a helicopter crash
